Pros and Cons Of Artificial Intelligence :

  • List of the Pros of Artificial Intelligence :

    •      1. Artificial intelligence completes routine tasks with ease.

         2. Artificial intelligence can work indefinitely.

         3. Artificial intelligence makes fewer errors.

         4. Artificial intelligence helps us to explore.

         5. Artificial intelligence can be used by anyone.

         6. Artificial intelligence makes us become more productive.

         7. Artificial intelligence could make us healthier.

         8. Artificial intelligence extends the human experience.

  • List of the Cons of Artificial Intelligence :

    •      1. Artificial intelligence comes with a steep price tag.

         2. Artificial intelligence will reduce employment opportunities.

         3. Artificial intelligence will be tasked with its own decisions.

         4. Artificial intelligence lacks creativity.

         5. Artificial intelligence can lack improvement.

         6. Artificial intelligence can be inaccurate.

         7. Artificial intelligence changes the power structure of societies.

         8. Artificial intelligence treats humanity as a commodity.
